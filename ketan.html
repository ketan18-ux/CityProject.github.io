Infrastructure & Network Optimization Projects

Algorithmic solutions built for planning roads, traffic, pipelines, routing and emergency systems in a smart city.

1. Optimal Road Network Construction

Algorithm: Minimum Spanning Tree (Kruskal / Prim)

Compute the minimum-cost road network connecting all major locations while avoiding redundant roads.

2. Shortest Travel-Time Routing

Algorithm: Dijkstra

Find the fastest route between important city zones based on weighted travel times.

3. Traffic Signal Timing Optimization

Algorithm: Scheduling / Greedy

Optimize traffic signal timings using peak hour flow to reduce waiting time at junctions.

4. Emergency Vehicle Fastest Route Planner

Algorithm: A* / Real-Time Dijkstra

Compute the quickest path for ambulances and fire services considering blocked/slow roads.

5. Water Pipeline Pressure Optimization

Algorithm: Max Flow / Min-Cut

Determine maximum water flow, detect bottlenecks and suggest reinforcement for critical pipes.

6. Power Grid Load Minimization

Algorithm: MST

Design efficient transformer connections with minimal loss and redundancy in the power network.

7. Garbage Truck Route Optimization

Algorithm: Travelling Salesman (TSP)

Plan shortest cyclic routes for waste collection vehicles covering all collection points.

8. Public Transport Timetable Generation

Algorithm: Interval Scheduling

Generate efficient bus schedules ensuring maximum coverage with limited buses.

9. Bridge Construction Priority Model

Algorithm: Min-Cut / Articulation Points

Identify critical bridges whose failure disconnects the city and prioritize them for strengthening.

10. Flood Evacuation Simulation

Algorithm: BFS Levels

Simulate flood spread, identify safe zones and fastest evacuation paths to shelters.

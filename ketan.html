Bucket 1 — Infrastructure & Network Optimization

These are my selected algorithmic problems related to city infrastructure, transportation, and network optimization.

My 10 Problems (Bucket 1)

1. Optimal Road Network Construction

Algorithm: Minimum Spanning Tree (Kruskal / Prim)

Given roads with construction cost, compute the minimum-cost connected city road network.

2. Shortest Travel-Time Routing

Algorithm: Dijkstra

Find the fastest route between any two zones or junctions in the smart city.

3. Traffic Signal Timing Optimization

Algorithm: Scheduling + Greedy

Optimize signal intervals using vehicle flow to reduce congestion.

4. Emergency Vehicle Fastest Route Planner

Algorithm: A* or Real-Time Dijkstra

Compute the quickest possible path for ambulances, fire trucks, etc.

5. Water Pipeline Pressure Optimization

Algorithm: Max Flow / Ford-Fulkerson

Find bottlenecks and compute maximum possible water flow in pipeline networks.

6. Power Grid Load Minimization

Algorithm: MST + Graph Optimization

Connect transformers with minimum energy loss and stable supply.

7. Garbage Truck Route Optimization

Algorithm: TSP (Nearest Neighbor, Christofides)

Find shortest route to collect waste across various city zones.

8. Public Transport Timetable Creation

Algorithm: Interval Scheduling

Generate optimal bus arrival schedules to cover more passengers.

9. Bridge Construction Priority Model

Algorithm: Min-Cut / Bridge Finding

Identify most critical edges whose failure breaks city connectivity.

10. Flood Evacuation Simulation

Algorithm: BFS Level Expansion

Compute safe reachable zones during flood emergencies.

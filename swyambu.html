Smart City, Environment & Sensor Analytics Projects

These problems focus on sustainability, pollution control, climate prediction, safety analytics, and environmental optimization.

1. Air Quality Prediction Network

Algorithm: Regression + DP Smoothing

Predict AQI values using historical multi-sensor data.

2. Smart Waste Segregation Automation

Algorithm: Rule-Based Decision Tree

Automatically classify waste into categories for recycling.

3. Noise Barrier Placement Optimization

Algorithm: Greedy / DP

Find minimum barriers needed to reduce sound pollution.

4. Flood-Susceptible Zone Detection

Algorithm: DFS + Elevation Mapping

Identify low-lying areas vulnerable to flooding.

5. Renewable Energy Distribution

Algorithm: Dynamic Programming

Distribute available solar/wind energy efficiently across the grid.

6. Health Risk Heatmap Generation

Algorithm: K-Means Clustering

Cluster disease incidents to identify medical risk hotspots.

7. Smart Irrigation Scheduling

Algorithm: Greedy / DP

Optimize water usage for parks and green zones.

8. CO₂ Offset Plantation Optimization

Algorithm: Knapsack

Maximize pollution reduction through trees within land limits.

9. Crime Hotspot Detection

Algorithm: Graph Centrality + Clustering

Identify high-crime zones and generate patrol loops.

10. Streetlight Fault Detection

Algorithm: Graph Traversal + Anomaly Check

Detect faulty streetlights using connected sensor data.

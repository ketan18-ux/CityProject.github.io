Introduction to DAA

Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) focuses on creating efficient and optimized solutions for real-world computational problems. It includes Divide & Conquer, Greedy Algorithms, Dynamic Programming, Graph Algorithms, and more.

How DAA Helps in City Design

✔ Graph Algorithms for road planning
✔ Greedy Algorithms for facility placement
✔ Dynamic Programming for cost optimization
✔ Search Algorithms for traffic routing

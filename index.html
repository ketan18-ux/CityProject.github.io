Introduction to DAA

Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) focuses on developing efficient, optimized solutions for computational problems. It helps engineers build systems that are fast, scalable, and cost-effective by using concepts like Divide & Conquer, Greedy Algorithms, Dynamic Programming, and Graph Theory.

How DAA Helps in City Design

City Design involves solving real-world problems where optimization is essential. Using DAA concepts, we can:

✔ Use **Graph Algorithms** to plan road maps and shortest routes.
✔ Use **Greedy Algorithms** for optimal placement of hospitals, parks, and fire stations.
✔ Use **Dynamic Programming** to minimize total cost of construction.
✔ Apply **Minimum Spanning Tree (MST)** for efficient zone connectivity.
✔ Use **Search Algorithms** for traffic simulation and routing.

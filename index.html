What is the course about?

Design and analysis of algorithms is the core of computer science, shaping how systems think and solve problems. The course focuses on understanding natural computational patterns and applying them to real-world challenges.

What kind of data structures and algorithms have you studied?

Our learning journey covered arrays, linked lists, stacks, queues, trees, graphs, and algorithmic techniques such as divide and conquer, greedy strategy, dynamic programming, and hashing.

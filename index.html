What is the course about?

Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) focuses on understanding the efficiency and structure of algorithms. It explores how algorithms solve real-world problems through optimal strategies, computational logic, and structured problem-solving techniques, forming the backbone of computer science applications.

What kind of data structures and algorithms have you studied?

We studied arrays, linked lists, stacks, queues, trees, graphs, hashing, and searching techniques. Algorithms such as sorting, dynamic programming, greedy algorithms, and shortest path calculations have enhanced our understanding of efficient computation.

How do you connect the course with real-time applications?

Algorithms guide GPS routing, data compression, search engines, and traffic management systems. These concepts enable smart city planning, automated decision-making, and optimized resource utilization for sustainable urban development.

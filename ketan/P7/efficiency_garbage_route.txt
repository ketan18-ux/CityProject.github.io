Efficiency Analysis — Garbage Truck Route Optimization (Problem 7)

Goal:
Compute a full distance matrix using Dijkstra and then construct a
near-optimal garbage collection route using the Nearest-Neighbour heuristic.

Definitions:
V = number of pickup points (20 in dataset)
E = number of connecting roads (20 in dataset)

--------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1 — Dijkstra from Every Node (All-Pairs Shortest Paths)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Each Dijkstra call uses:
    O(E log V)

We call Dijkstra V times:
    O(V * E log V)

Space:
    dist array per run: O(V)
    adjacency list:      O(V + E)

--------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 2 — Build Distance Matrix
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Fill V × V table:
    O(V^2)

Magnitude small compared to Dijkstra step.

--------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 3 — Greedy TSP Route (Nearest Neighbour)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
At each step, choose nearest unvisited node:
    Worst case: check V nodes per step → O(V)
    V steps total → O(V^2)

Space:
    visited array:  O(V)
    route array:    O(V)

--------------------------------------------------------------------
OVERALL TIME COMPLEXITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Dijkstra (dominant):  O(V * E log V)
Distance matrix:      O(V^2)
Greedy route:         O(V^2)

Total:
    O(V * E log V)

For V = 20, E = 20 → extremely fast in real-time.

--------------------------------------------------------------------
SPACE COMPLEXITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Adjacency list:        O(V + E)
Distance matrix:       O(V^2)
Route storage:         O(V)

Total:
    O(V^2)

--------------------------------------------------------------------
Conclusion:
Algorithm is highly efficient for medium-size smart-city routing.
Provides near-optimal garbage truck sequence using lightweight heuristics.

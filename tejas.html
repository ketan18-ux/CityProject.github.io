Land Use, Zoning & Location Optimization Projects

These problems involve spatial allocation, clustering, matching, zoning, and optimal city planning using algorithms.

1. Optimal Hospital Location

Algorithm: K-Means / k-Medians

Place hospitals to minimize average distance for citizens.

2. School Allocation to Students

Algorithm: Bipartite Matching

Match students to nearest valid schools with minimal travel distance.

3. Smart Parking Slot Allocation

Algorithm: Hungarian Algorithm

Assign cars to parking slots with minimum total walking distance.

4. Industrial vs Residential Separation

Algorithm: Graph Coloring

Assign zone colors ensuring harmful industries don’t touch residential areas.

5. Public Park Placement Optimization

Algorithm: Max Coverage

Select k park spots maximizing total population served.

6. Land Subdivision Optimization

Algorithm: Divide & Conquer

Divide large land plot into optimal smaller usable plots.

7. High Footfall Commercial Zone Prediction

Algorithm: Clustering

Cluster regions to predict future commercial hotspots.

8. Creation of Green Belt Buffers

Algorithm: Constraint Solver

Maximize green buffers around city limits with minimal relocation.

9. Footpath Connectivity Plan

Algorithm: Graph Connectivity

Identify disconnected pedestrian paths & repair with minimal new footpaths.

10. Underground Utility Routing

Algorithm: Disjoint Shortest Paths

Lay sewer, fiber, and power lines without crossing each other.
